My Journey

Hi, I'm Gauri, a BCA student at S.S. College in Shahjahanpur, Uttar Pradesh. While pursuing my degree in computer applications, I discovered my true passion ‚Äì charcoal art.

What started as a hobby during college breaks has transformed into a fulfilling journey of over 2 years. I fell in love with the depth, texture, and emotion that charcoal brings to portraits. There's something magical about transforming a blank sheet into a lifelike portrait using just charcoal and paper.

Today, I create custom charcoal portraits for clients across India, capturing precious moments and memories in timeless artwork. Each portrait is crafted with patience, attention to detail, and a deep commitment to bringing out the essence of the subject.

Beyond creating art, I'm passionate about teaching. I believe everyone has an artist within them, and I love helping students discover and develop their charcoal drawing skills. Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to refine your techniques, I'm here to guide you with personalized attention and encouragement.
